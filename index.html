Your 30-Day Abs Challenge Guide

This guide is designed to progressively challenge your core over 30 days. Remember that nutrition, hydration, and consistency are key. Listen to your body and rest when you need to.

Important Considerations

Warm-Up (5 mins)

  • Jumping Jacks: 60 seconds
  • High Knees: 60 seconds
  • Torso Twists: 30s per side
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 60 seconds
  • Leg Swings: 30s per leg

Cool-Down (5 mins)

  • Cobra Stretch: 30 seconds
  • Child's Pose: 60 seconds
  • Lying Spinal Twist: 30s per side
  • Knees-to-Chest: 60 seconds

Nutrition & Hydration

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Focus on lean protein, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables. A calorie deficit is necessary to reveal abdominal muscles; this plan builds them, your diet makes them visible.
